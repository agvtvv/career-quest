FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need any technical skills?

None at all. If you can answer questions about your own business, you can use Frontage. There's no drag-and-drop editor to learn.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes. Connect a domain you already own, or buy a new one during setup — either way, it's a couple of clicks.

What if I already have some photos and branding?

Upload what you have and Frontage will use it. If you don't have any photos yet, we'll use relevant stock imagery until you do.

How much will it cost?
